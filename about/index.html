Feng-Ting Liao

I am a Senior Research Scientist at MediaTek Research, working on large language models, deep reinforcement learning in chip design, speech technology, and representation learning with publications at ICML, EACL, ASRU.

With a team of talented researchers, I developed BreeXe, a SOTA commercialized LLM beating GPT3.5 on both knowledge and chat capabilities in Traditional Chinese, and opensourced Breeze (>11k downloads) for supporting research and entrepreneural efforts in Traditional Chinese speaking communities.
